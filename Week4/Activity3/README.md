# Activity 3 for week 4

## User case 1:

1. Customer enters user information and registers.

2. Bank staff verify the customer's information and open the first account for the user.

3. Customer can open another account through the exchange system.

4. Customer deposits into the accounts.

## User case 2:

1. Customer exchanges money between different accounts.

2. Customer queries the balance of each account and confirms the exchange result.

3. Customer withdrawal from the account.

4. Customer asks some questions, and the bank staff respond to them.
